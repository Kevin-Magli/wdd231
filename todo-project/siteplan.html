Site Plan Wireframe

Site Name

Magli's To-Do List

Site Purpose

To have a cool dynamic To-Do List that will be integrated futurely in my personal all-purpose website. This To-Do List will have all sorts of functions that will work differently from other normal To-Do list apps and will interact with other parts of my website, again in the future. For this project it'll just be a really neat To-Do List App :)

Scenarios

Can i create my tasks in different groups of tasks?

Yes you can! By default we'll have 3 boxes called Backlog, Doing and Done, but you can rename it to other things you like. You can even create more!

Can i mouse drag the tasks around?

Yes you can! Just drag and drop it wherever you like.

Can i set a completion date to my tasks?

Yes you can! And the info will say how much time is left for it's completion.

Color Schema

Basically, not applied here but the background will be a space picture, the black and white for contrast for shapes, the red for accent, orange for the tasks close to completion and green for completed tasks.

Typography

Will be the Exo 2 google font.
