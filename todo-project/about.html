About

The Magli's in the name of the app is my Last name "Magliocchi". This is the first of many applications created to compose my personal all-in-one website. I always find the To-do apps to be "too much" and it made me get distracted by their million functionalities (or even be disappointed when i don't have the specific feature i wanted). So if you're disappointed with something, just do it yourself!

Why the app

This app and website are being made for the Final Project of my WDD 231 Byu Course. The idea was having an app with useful functionalities that i could even use in my personal life if i wanted to. Pretty neat stuff!

The sad part is that there we're too many ideas for too little time (i'm even late lol) but i wish this meets the requirements. I plan on expanding the functionalities of this app (and more in the future) to be part of a big All-in-One personal website.

If you want to contribute, share your ideas! I'll gladly listen to them and every good idea will make it into this app.
